OV MEDICAL EQUIPMENT

The OV Medical Equipment company was established in 2022 as a necessity following the changes in the field of health policy in Romania, succeeding through promptness and professionalism to win a constant and numerous clientele. The quality of the medical services offered, as well as the financial performance of our clients from the public and private sectors, depend on the provision of high quality services from us, both in the period preceding the purchase, as well as during the installation, warranty and post-warranty periods.

OV Medical Equipment is the exclusive partner on the Romanian market of several companies producing medical equipment from Europe, Asia and North America and is constantly looking for new valuable partners. The commercial department is constantly developing to be able to satisfy all the demands of our customers. Along with the commercial side, the service activity was and is an emblematic activity, being given special attention.

Since we are a dynamic company of integrated services, we offer you our experience in the field to find together the solution to all your problems.

Our goal is to improve the quality of life by offering viable and efficient solutions.
